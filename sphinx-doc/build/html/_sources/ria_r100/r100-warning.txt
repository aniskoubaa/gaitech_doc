==============================
Warning and safety precautions
==============================

.. image:: images/warning.jpg
    :align: left
    :width: 150pt


* It is warned to turnoff robot while charging.
* While using joystick to control robot rise throttle slowly. Sudden rise may lead robot move in jerks at start. This may affect components placed over robot.
* While main switch turned ON never access internal hardware components.
* It is always suggested to make E-stop enabled while robot is at steady state.
* Never unplug battery while robot turned ON.
