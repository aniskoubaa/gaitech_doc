Electronic Speed Controller (ESC) Calibration
---------------------------------------------

Electronic speed controllers are responsible for spinning the motors at the speed requested by the autopilot. Most ESCs need to be calibrated so that they know the minimum and maximum pwm values that the flight controller will send. Following video provides instructions for calibrating ESCs.
